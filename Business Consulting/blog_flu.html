Healthy Habits for the Flu Season

  • Tips, Flu Season

As the seasons change and temperatures drop, the risk of catching the seasonal flu or common cold rises significantly. While over-the-counter medications can treat the symptoms, prevention is always better than a cure.

Building a robust immune system should be your first line of defense. Incorporating Vitamin C into your daily routine is highly recommended. Fresh citrus fruits like oranges and lemons are excellent natural sources, but high-quality Vitamin C supplements and effervescent tablets can provide that extra boost when your diet falls short. You can find a wide range of immune boosters right here at Kadel Pharmacy.

"A strong immune system is built daily. Don't wait until you are sick to start taking care of your body."

Aside from vitamins, hydration and rest are deeply underrated. Drinking plenty of water helps flush toxins from your system, while getting 7 to 8 hours of sleep allows your body to repair and strengthen its natural defenses. Sleep deprivation severely impairs your immune system, making you highly susceptible to circulating viruses.

Finally, never underestimate the power of basic hygiene. Washing your hands frequently with soap and water, or using an alcohol-based hand sanitizer when on the go, drastically reduces the transmission of flu viruses. If you do start feeling under the weather, drop by our pharmacy. We can recommend the best decongestants, cough syrups, and pain relievers to get you back on your feet quickly.
